The Method

EestiTalk is a platform that provides a guide to learn Estonian up to A2 level, in order to acquire this level you should follow the guidelines and do the exercises presented.

If interested there's also a list of extra sources of information in order to acquire a higher level of dominancy in this language.

Grammar

We will learn how to learn the Estonian grammar first by learning how the fundamental parts of the speech work, such as nouns, pronouns, adjectives, articles...

We will look for patterns in the casual conversations that may happen if you're stuck in Tallinn and we'll practice the verb forms.

Vocabulary

Flash cards will be the main way to study vocabulary, which has been proven to be an effective way to learn the vocabulary of the language you're interested about.

We'll also learn how to create new sentences with the words you find difficult to learn, you'll get used to using it in a sentence and thus will be more likely to remember the word.

Guidelines & Resources

We offer a selected variety of resources and methods to learn the language, such as inmersion and ways to associate the new vocabulary you have learned with objects or situations that you could deal in your daily life.
