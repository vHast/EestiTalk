Please & Thank you

These are some common expressions to express gratitude.

Imagine that you've been given an item as a present, there's different grade sof appreciation that you could express.

Aitäh.

Thank you.

Sur aitäh

Thanks a lot!

Sur sur aitäh

Thanks you so much!!

Sometimes you will be handed out an item, for example, purchasing an item in a supermarket, here's how the conversation should be.

Palun

Here you are

Aitäh

Thank you!

Pole tänu väärt!

Don't mention it.
