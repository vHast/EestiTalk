Hello!

First, we are going to learn the ways to say hello!

These are the basic expressions to greet someone, hover on the word or click to receive the answer.

Good morning!

Tere hommikust!

Good afternoon!

Tere päevast!

Good evening!

Tere õhtust!

Here are the days of the week, note that the name of the day goes always before (paev), except on Friday (Reede)

In order to say goodbye we can say the next expressions

Head aega!

Goodbye!

Nägemist!

See you!

Kõike head!

All the best!

Kohtumiseni

So long!

Kuulmiseni

Will hear from you

Tsau!

Bye (Informal)

In order to say goodbye at a certain time of the day, we can choose the next expressions.

Head päeva!

Have a good day!

Head õhtut!

Have a good evening!

Head õõd!

Have a good night!

If we know when we are going to see each other again, the easiest way to work out these expressions is to learn the time of the day/day and then select the right word, here are a few examples.

Ohteni!

Till the evening!

Homseni!

Till tomorrow!

Ulehumseni!

Till the day after tomorrow!

The same can be applied if we know when know the day that we are going to see each other.

Note that all you have to do is to put the day first and add -ani after the name of the day, except on Friday (Reede).

Esmaspäevani

Till Monday!

Teisipäevani

Till Tuesday!

Kolmapäevani

Till Wednesday!

Neljapäevani

Till Thursday!

Reedeni

Til Friday!

Laupäevani

Till Saturday!

Puhapäevani
