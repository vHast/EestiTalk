Do you speak Estonian?

Here are a few expressions that could be useful if you don't know what to say or if you dont understand what the other person is saying.

Kas te raagite eesti keelt?

Do you (formal) speak Estonian?

Räägin küll

Yes, I do

Ei Räägi

I don't

Here's the informal way to do it.

Kas sa räägid ukraina keelt?

Do you (informal) speak Ukrainian?

Jah, natukene.

Yes, a little.

And another one

Kas sa oskad inglise keelt?

Do you (informal) speak English?

Kahjuks ei oska. Aga sina?

No, unfortunately I don't. What about you? (informal)

Mina ka ei oska

I don't speak it either

And here's a situatonal one, if you don't understand a single word.

Kas te oskate eesti kelt?

Do you (formal) speak Estonian?

Vabandust, ma ei saa aru

I'm sorry, I don't understand.

It would be a good idea to ask the person if he speaks English (kas te raagite inglise keelt?) after you've told the person that you don't understand the language.
