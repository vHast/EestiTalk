Getting acquainted

These are some common expressions to know when you're meeting someone new or being introduced to someone.

Tere! Mina olen Epp!

Hello! I'm Epp!

Mina olen Tiina, tema on Peeter, ja see on Muki

I'm Tiina, he is Peeter, and this is Muki

Oi, kui nunnu koer!

Oh, what a cute dog!

Here's a situation where you're being introduced to somebody.
