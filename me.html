Me / I

These are some common expressions to take orders and do basic actions.

We start by indicating a basic action such as 'Come here'

Come here!

Tule siia!

I'll be right there!

Kohe tulen!

We can also add "please"(palun) to add a grade of gratitude in the action.

Please take a seat!

Istu palun!

Where should I sit?

Kuhu ma istun?

And, now we can also make pleads

Please, call me then!

Heliista siis mulle palun!

Ok, I will call!

Olgu, helistan!
