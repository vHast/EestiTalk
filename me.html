Me / I

These are some common expressions to express actions that you personally do (first person).

Ma soon

I eat, I will eat, I do eat

Ma joon

I drink, I will drink, I do drink

Ma istun

I sit, I will sit, I'm sitting

Ma tulen

I come, I'm coming, I do come

We can play with the ending in order to alter the context of the action.

Ma söön

I eat, I will eat, I do eat

In this case [n] is added to the word to indicate that we do the action, if we remove the letter we get the imperative version of the word

Söö

Eat!

And if we add [ära] we create the negative command

Ära söö

Don't eat!
